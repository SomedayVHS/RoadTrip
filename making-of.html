Notes de production

Making-of

Analog imperfection is intentional
Act I — The Departure
01Last Bag
Cinematic still, 2.39:1 cinemascope framing, Kodak Vision3 250D emulation, static tripod shot. Pre-dawn suburban driveway, cool blue-grey light. A weathered vintage car trunk open, a woman with mid-length red hair loading a worn leather duffel bag, seen from behind, her face turned away. Long shadows, dew on the car's chrome trim. Malick-inspired negative space, muted cool color grade, fine analog grain, slight lens vignette.
02Two Doors
Cinematic still, 2.39:1 cinemascope, Kodak Vision3 250D, static wide shot. A man with three-day beard stands by the driver's door, hand resting on the roof, looking down the empty street rather than at camera. The redhead woman is a soft blur entering the passenger side. Cool dawn palette, desaturated blues and greys, suggestion rather than frontality, analog grain, soft halation on the rising sun at frame edge.
03First Light on the Windshield
Cinematic still, 2.39:1 cinemascope, Kodak Vision3 250D. Interior car shot through windshield, dashboard slightly out of focus. Reflection of empty road ahead. Faint outlines of the couple, mostly silhouetted against the low sun. Cool-to-warm gradient sky, subtle lens flare, fine grain, static camera mimicking a mounted dash rig.
Act II — The Road
04The Straight Line
Cinematic still, 2.39:1 cinemascope, Kodak Vision3 250D, static tripod, extreme wide shot. A single straight desert highway vanishing to the horizon under a bleached turquoise sky. The car is small in frame, a speck of rust-red against ochre desert. Heat shimmer visible above the asphalt. Minimal, Malick-style negative space, muted warm-cool grade, subtle analog imperfections.
05Gas Station Ghost
Cinematic still, 2.39:1 cinemascope, Kodak Vision3 250D. Abandoned roadside gas station, faded signage, peeling paint. The bearded man leans against a pump, filling the tank, looking off-frame. The redhead woman is seen through the car's open door, legs out, sunhat over her face. Hard midday contre-jour, dust particles visible in the light shafts, warm ochre and rust tones, fine grain.
06Windshield Reflection
Cinematic still, 2.39:1 cinemascope, Kodak Vision3 250D. Close static shot on the car's side mirror, reflecting the woman's red hair whipping in the wind, her expression calm and distant. Desert blur in the background. Hard contre-jour, warm highlights, cool shadow falloff, visible grain and slight chromatic fringing at frame edges.
07Two Silhouettes, One Horizon
Cinematic still, 2.39:1 cinemascope, Kodak Vision3 250D, wide static shot. The car stopped on the shoulder, both figures standing at a distance from it, backs to camera, looking at the vast empty horizon. Heat haze distorting the ground line. Muted warm-cool grade, strong negative space above the horizon, Malick-inspired composition, fine grain.
Act III — The Camp
08Dunes at Dusk
Cinematic still, 2.39:1 cinemascope, Kodak Vision3 500T emulation, static tripod. Rolling desert dunes at dusk, deep blue-violet sky fading to amber at the horizon line. Small tent silhouette and the couple's shapes in the middle distance, unposed, caught mid-gesture. Increased grain structure typical of 500T stock, cool-warm contrast, soft shadow detail.
09Firelight
Cinematic still, 2.39:1 cinemascope, Kodak Vision3 500T, static shot, low angle. A small campfire in the foreground, out of focus, warm orange glow lighting the edges of two figures seated close but not touching, faces mostly in shadow. Star-flecked dark blue sky above. Heavy but intentional analog grain, warm-cool contrast, "analog imperfection is intentional."
10Her Hair in Firelight
Cinematic still, 2.39:1 cinemascope, Kodak Vision3 500T, close static shot. The redhead woman's profile lit only by firelight, hair catching warm orange highlights against a near-black background. Soft focus falloff, visible film grain, halation around the brightest highlights, intimate but restrained framing — face turned three-quarters away from camera.
Act IV — The Horizon
11Leaving, Again
Cinematic still, 2.39:1 cinemascope, Kodak Vision3 250D, wide static shot, camera far from the subjects. Pale dawn desert, faint ground mist. The car pulling away down the straight road, two small silhouettes visible through the rear window. Desaturated palette, minimal color apart from a faint warm sun glow at the horizon. Malick-style vast negative space, fine analog grain, slight vignette — the frame holds the emptiness longer than the car.
